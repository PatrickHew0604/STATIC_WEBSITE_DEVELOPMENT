Sport Badminton Carnival

APU Sports Carnival 2022 is an annual event organised by APU Student Representative Council (SRC) which cultivate APU students to be active in sports other than hectic assignments.
APU Badminton Club is honored to be part of the community assisting on making the fun carnival to happen. You can join the Sport Carnival for fun with your friends or be a competitive athlete aiming for a glory title in different sports 🎾! Join us now and have fun with your friends 😄 !

Event Details :
📆 Date : 27th & 28th August 2022
⏰ Time : 9am - 7pm (First Day) & 9am-2pm (Semi-Final & Final)
📍 Venue : Prima Badminton Center

Purple League APU BC Badminton Clinic

A badminton clinic event in collaboration of Asia Pacific University Badminton Club and Malaysia Purple League.The event is aimed to increase the awareness 🚨 of the importance of sports and badminton in particular among the youths in Malaysia. During the event, the participants would meet and learn from some of the best badminton coaches and players in Malaysia in hopes that these elite coaches and athletes could serve as a role model for these participants and also to elevate the level of badminton in the country indirectly.

Competition Details :
📆 Date : 4th May 2019
⏰ Time : 10am - 12pm
📍 Venue : Pioneer Badminton Centre, Endah Parade
