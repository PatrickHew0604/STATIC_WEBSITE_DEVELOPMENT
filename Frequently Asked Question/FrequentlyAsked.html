Frequently Asked Questions(FAQs)

How can I join the club ?

Check the member registration form at our official website for more information.

What is the cost of membership?

There are no additional fees or expenses for participating in club events or activities, as all costs are covered by the membership fee which need to be paid while joining the club which cost $10.

What is the club's policy on safety ?

The APU badminton club prioritizes the safety of our members and requires all participants to follow established safety guidelines during practices and matches. These guidelines include wearing appropriate athletic gear and shoes, using proper technique and form during gameplay, and being aware of your surroundings on the court.

Is the club suitable for beginners?

We have players of all skill levels in our club, and we organize events and training sessions to cater to each level. Beginners are welcome and will find plenty of opportunities to improve their game.

What equipment do I need ?

To play badminton, you will need a few key pieces of equipment. The most important piece is a badminton racket, which can be purchased from sporting goods stores or online retailers. You may also want to consider getting a pair of badminton shoes with non-marking soles, which can help you move more easily on the court. In addition, wearing comfortable athletic clothing that allows you to move freely is recommended. The club does not provide equipment , so it is the responsibility of each member to obtain their own equipment before attending practices or matches.

Does the club participate in tournaments?

Yes, the university badminton club participates in various tournaments throughout the year, both within the university and outside of it. These tournaments provide opportunities for our members to compete against other badminton players and clubs, and to showcase their skills. We encourage all of our members to participate in tournaments, regardless of their skill level, as it is a great way to gain experience and improve your game. Information about upcoming tournaments will be communicated to members via email and through our website, so be sure to check those regularly for updates.
